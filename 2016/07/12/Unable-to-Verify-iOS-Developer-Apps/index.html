Unable to Verify iOS Developer Apps

Jul 12, 2016

As of this morning, https://ppq.apple.com is down and developers are unable to verify their development apps on their iOS devices.

I was initially confused by this error since my iOS device had a network connection.

After some quick googling, I found that the site https://ppq.apple.com needs to be up in order for developers to verify applications. The error message should be updated to reflect that…

See stackoverflow thread here.
