Unmasking Masked URL Redirect Records

Apr 13, 2016

TIL that a “masked” url redirect record in your DNS config most likely means your hosting provider is placing your website inside of an iframe.

I noticed this because all of my <meta> tags were missing and the page was not scaling appropriately on mobile devices due to a missing viewport meta tag.
