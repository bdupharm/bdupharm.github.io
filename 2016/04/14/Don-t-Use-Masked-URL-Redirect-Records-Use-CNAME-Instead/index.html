Don't Use Masked URL Redirect Records, Use CNAME Instead

Apr 14, 2016

A “masked” url redirect record in your DNS config most likely means your hosting provider is placing your website inside of an iframe.

No magic here… your page now looks like this:

Notice that all the important <meta> tags like the viewport tag for mobile responsiveness are missing (actually just loaded into the iframe).

Save yourself and just use a CNAME record instead. A CNAME record is a name to name resolution and maps directly to the desired domain.
